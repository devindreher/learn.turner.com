LeadershipNow

A suite of virtual and classroom offerings open to anyone with people leader responsibilities — either on an org chart or in practice. Each offering is aligned to one or more of our leadership traits:

Accountable.

Turner leaders design strategic and actionable plans, lead teams that deliver results, drive performance with actionable feedback, and trust and empower their teams to deliver.

Agile.

Turner leaders are iterative and flexible. They promote a constant state of reinvention, build teams that thrive on innovation, and grow and nurture team members.

Collaborative.

Turner leaders break down silos and build partnerships. They seek diverse perspectives, think and act globally, and make proactive communications a priority.

Innovative.

Being a Turner leader involves taking initiative and embracing risks. It means encouraging bold decision making and hiring and fostering an environment where creative thinking is the norm. Turner leaders challenge their teams to focus on priorities while encourage the shedding of non-essential work.
