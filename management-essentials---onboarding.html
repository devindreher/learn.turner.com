Onboarding

Onboarding at Turner is a process that happens over an extended period of time. It lays the groundwork for new employees to become productive and successful team members for the long haul.

Employees who experience effective onboarding processes are 58% more likely to be with the organization after 3 years. Research suggests that managers have the number one impact on employees' overall onboarding experience.
