Unleashing Your A-Team

The construct of what we call a "team" comes in all shapes and sizes -- each with its own unique set of challenges.

We'll examine the set of ingredients which distinguish a truly high-performing team and the kinds of leadership actions which instill these behaviors in the team you lead.
You will:
- Learn the value and role effective teamwork plays in development organizational agility
- Understand the unique characteristics and how our 4 attributes (accountability, agility, collaboration, and innovation) play out in your team
- Understand and apply the leadership style that best suits your team
