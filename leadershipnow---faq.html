LeadershipNow FAQ

What is LeadershipNow?

The LeadershipNow suite is comprised of self-directed learning experiences for people leaders delivered in 2-hour interactive webinars and 3-hour face-to-face modules.



Who can participate?

Anyone with people leader responsibilities are welcome. This even includes those of us who have informal leadership responsibilities such as project leads.



How is LeadershipNow different than past leadership development programs at Turner?

LeadershipNow is a self-directed, self-selected program encompassing an array of virtual and classroom offerings.

We designed LeadershipNow to offer robust learning experiences that are customized for the uniqueness for Turner while, at the same time, making it more accessible for those in locations across the globe.

Classroom modules are scheduled based upon demand. Reach out to Lisa Sims in Turner People Development if you're interested in offering a session in your location.



Is manager approval required to participate?

No. If you have people leader responsibilities and have been at Turner for at least one year, you're welcome to participate.

We do recommend having an ongoing conversation with your manager about your development and participation in LeadershipNOW as well as any development aspirations you have.



What's covered in the program?

LeadershipNOW focuses on leadership attributes that will drive the change we need now. These include being a leader, being accountable and executing, being innovative, and being agile.

Refer to the module descriptions for more information.



If I previously attended a leadership development program, can I participate in LeadershipNow?

Yes.

We also encourage you to have a conversation with your manager and consider their feedback as a valuable component to the development opportunities you consider.
