Coaches

"Coaching is about performing at your best through the assistance of someone that will challenge, stimulate, and guide you to keep growing." - Gerard O'Donovan

What is coaching?

  • A process of self-discovery
  • The continued development of people leaders
  • Forward-thinking, facilitated breakthroughs to prepare leaders for successful career transitions
  • A business investment with specific objectives for a limited time
  • An accountable partnership between the key individuals involved (coach, mentee, and manager)

What isn't coaching?

  • Life coaching (not that we're against it, we just don't do it)
  • Conducted to address performance problems or to "coach someone out of the organization"
  • An ongoing arrangement for multiple years (this isn't therapy)
  • For all employees — we just don't have those sorts of resources
