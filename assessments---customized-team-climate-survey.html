Customized Team Climate Survey

The Team Climate Survey is a free method for assessing your team's culture.
If you need to get a feel around specifics needs or gaps on your team, Turner People Development can help create a climate survey to tap into the thoughts and feelings of employees on your team.
Because this is a survey developed internally, we're able to customize the content to fit your needs.
After compiling the data, we'll work with your one-on-one to identify trends and patterns. We'll also help create customized solutions aimed at solving any issues that arise from the data in order to increase your team's effectiveness.
GET STARTED:
You must work with your Human Resources Generalist and/or Turner People Development to use this tool for your leadership development.
Send us a note if you're interested.
