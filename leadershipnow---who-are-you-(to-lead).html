Who Are You (to Lead)?

Increasing self-awareness in our professional lives brings great benefits and is a predictor of overall success.

In this interactive webinar, you'll take a significant step towards becoming the leader you want to be by investing the time to actively reflect on what type of leader you are, what motivates you, and any areas you want to improve to better understand those you lead.
You will:
- Understand how the beliefs and values we hold impact how we behave as leaders and shape results
- Realize the benefits that come from being more self-aware in the workplace
- Identify distinctive attributes of your leadership style better utilize self-development opportunities in your work to increase self-knowledge
