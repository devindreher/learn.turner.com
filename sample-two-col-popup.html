Leaders need to be able to provide a culture in which innovation and creativity and thrive.

This 3-hour classroom session looks at the philosophies and practices that guide the leadership of creative teams. A case-study leveraging the book Creativity, Inc. is used to explore what goes into building a creative culture and then applied to our environment at Turner.

You will:
- Understand implications of creativity and innovation on leadership and teams
- Learn what characteristics and behaviors distinguish leaders in a creative environment
- Discover how to support your team in a creative culture and apply lessons from case studies to our environment
