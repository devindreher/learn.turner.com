Coaching Your Kickass Team

One of the primary roles of a leader is to ensure the performance and development of the people that report to them.

This 3-hour classroom session covers the essential skills required to be an effective coach, including a structured coaching model and use of interactive activities to ensure skill mastery.

You will:
- Identify coaching needs for your team and guide them to higher levels of effectiveness by actively engaging in dialog
- Learn how to hold a coaching conversation and provide feedback, insights, and guidance while encouraging ownership in the solution and
action plan - Apply coaching skills through role-playing actual Turner scenarios
