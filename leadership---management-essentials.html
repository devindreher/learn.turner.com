MANAGEMENT ESSENTIALS

Great people leaders bring out the very best in their people. They maximize their potential and create a thriving team environment focused on common objectives.

As a people leader, your key responsibilities are to motivate, develop, and mentor your team. How you accomplish this can show up in different ways.

This section gives you a framework with ideas an opportunities for leading a team.

A large part of a manager's role focuses on people who make up the team. Use this framework as a guide to categorizing your role in supporting yours. You have many opportunities to influence your team's experience in every phase of their career at Turner.
