WICT Betsy Magness Leadership Institute

This is an educational journey that focuses on core leadership competencies, including communication effectiveness and organizational influence, driving and implementing change, creating engagement, driving results, and executing organizational vision. Program fellows emerge better equipped to lead in times of constant change and with a committed passion to share what they've learned with their teams, companies, and industry at-large.
