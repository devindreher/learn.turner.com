Betsy Magness Leadership Institute

This is an educational journey that focuses on core leadership competencies, including communication effectiveness and organizational influence, driving and implementing change, creating engagement, driving results, and executing organizational vision.
Program fellows emerge better equipped to lead in times of constant change and with a committed passion to share what they've learned with their teams, companies, and industry at-large.
UPCOMING SESSIONS:
- September 2017 - May 2018 (13 days total)
- Applications typically accepted from mid-November to mid-February
WHO IS THIS FOR?
Female VPs and above.
LOGISTICS:
- Various U.S. cities
- $17,500 + $165 application fee

Program costs are subject to change and do not include travel/expenses unless otherwise noted. Your department must fund any associated costs. Space is limited in each program -- your full commitment and participation is expected.
If you need to withdraw after registering, be sure to check the program's cancellation policy. Depending on when you cancel, it may be too late to offer your space to another Turner colleague. Check with your manager and HR Generalist before canceling.

INQUIRE This program requires nomination and selection.
Turner People Development, HR, and senior leaders work
together to nominate and select participants.
