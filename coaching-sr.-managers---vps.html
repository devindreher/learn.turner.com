Coaches: Sr. Managers — VPs

Step What Happens
Step 1: Identify the Need You or an executive sponsor identify a need for an internal certified coach.

Your leader discusses and initiates the coaching request with your HR partner.

Your HR partner informs Turner People Development of specific coaching needs, desired outcomes, situational context, personality, and location.

If an assessment or leadership program element is needed, we have certified HR coaches in a variety of assessments
Step 2: Select a Coach If possible, Turner People Development provides two potential coach bios based on location, need, and coaching expertise.

You then interview potential coaches for the best fit for your needs.

Turner People Development facilitates informal contracting between you and your coach for centralized tracking and accountability.

Your time with your coach is typically 3-6 months.
Step 3: Engage! You and your coach begin regular meetings - usually twice per month - and finalize objectives early on while sharing with your executive sponsor/leader and HR partner.

Check-in meetings are held at the beginning and end of the engagement with your leader.

Throughout this time, standard coaching practices and confidentiality is expected.
Step 4: Close & Follow-Up Your coach contacts Turner People Development to close the engagement and confirm completion.

A final meeting takes place between you, your coach, and your manager.

Exit surveys are completed by you and your coach.

Turner People Development follows up with a 6 month check-in with you HR partner to discuss sustained behavior change and encourages check-ins with your leader on your progress.
