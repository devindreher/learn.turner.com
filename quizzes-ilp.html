Inclusive Leader Profile

Individual Contributor Edition

The Inclusive Leader Profile (ILP) is based on 20 years of information gathered in one-on-one interviews, as well as focus groups and thousands of diversity training sessions.

The ILP© is about the subtle things people do, or don't do, that lead those who are diverse or to feel isolated, powerless, or in any way "less valued."

The ILP© is based on the belief that most people don't intend to discriminate, judge, or isolate others. It often happens without intent, but the impact can be devastating to individuals and an organization. Taking this "quiz" will give you an opportunity to evaluate your behaviors and to offer you awareness of how to modify them in order to be more respectful of all people.

 

How often do I, as an individual...

Challenge others when they make racial/ethnic/sexually derogatory comments?

 
 

Speak up when someone is humiliating another person or acting inappropriately?

 
 

Think about the impact of my comments and actions before I speak or act?

 
 

Refuse to participate in jokes that are derogatory to any individual group, culture, gender, or sexual orientation?

 
 

Refrain from repeating statements or rumors that reinforce prejudice or bias?

 
 

Consider reality before repeating or believing rumors or assumptions about anyone?

 
 

Recognize and challenge the biases that affect my own thinking?

 
 

Avoid using language that reinforces negative stereotypes?

 
 

Assume that culturally diverse individuals are as skilled and competent as others?

 
 

Get to know people from different cultures and groups as individuals?

 
 

Realize people of other cultures have a need to support each other and to connect as a group?

 
 

Accept and reinforce the fact that not everyone has to act or look a certain way to be successful or valuable as an employee.

 
 

Take responsibility for helping new people in our organization, including people from different cultures, feel welcomed and accepted?

 
 

Include culturally diverse individuals in informal networks and/or social events?

 
 

Learn about, appreciate the richness of other cultures, and honor their holidays and events?

 
 

Act inclusively toward colleagues of all countries, cultures, and differences?

 
 

Encourage culturally diverse individuals to speak out on their issues and concerns and treat those issues as valid?

 
 

Encourage culturally diverse individuals to take risks?

 
 

Listen to the ideas of culturally diverse individuals and give them credit, both one-on-one and in group meetings?

 
 

Include culturally diverse individuals in team decision-making processes that affect them?

 
 

Think of different perspectives, other than mine, as beneficial, valuable, and worthwhile?

 
 

Provide straightforward and timely feedback to culturally diverse individuals?

 
 

Share the written and unwritten rules of our organizational culture with those who are not aware of them?

 
 

Avoid assuming that others will or should act/react in the same way I would act/react?

 
 

Take personal responsibility for helping my organization meet its diversity goals?

 
 

Follow and reinforce organizational policies regarding equal treatment, including having courageous conversations with others who violate these policies?

 
 

Consider culturally diverse individuals for all opportunities over which I have influence?

 
 

Keep others, including culturally diverse individuals, in the information loops, both formal and informal?

 
 

Seek opportunities to connect with others and build rapport?

 
 

Watch for cultural differences in body language and listen to what may be said or implied about differences in perception?

 
 

Make the extra effort to help my organization retain culturally diverse individuals?

 
 

Reinforce others for behaviors that support cultural diversity?

 
 

Question organizational policies and procedures that I believe lead to the exclusion of anyone?

 
 

Address disagreements or misunderstandings, assuming the other person is well intended?

 
 

Do all I can to help create a work environment where all employees and customers are respected and valued?
