Stop Riding the Fence (It Can't Be Comfortable)

As a leader, you must be able to weigh a number of elements when making a decision.

That can be tough. But, we'll help you understand the range of decision styles, an awareness of your decision biases, tools and techniques useful for prioritization, and ways to improve your ability to engage others in making key decisions.
You will:
- Understand the most common decision biases and how to avoid them
- Apply tools and techniques to improve both individual and group decision making
- Learn a prioritization rubric for deciding what to do, delete, defer, or do differently
