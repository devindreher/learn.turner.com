Bold Hiring

Use When:

You find yourself on autopilot during the hiring process.

You want to position your team (and Turner) for the future.

You want to create an atmosphere of inclusion that values differences and leverages the diversity of others to win the war on talent.

Logistics:

— 2 Hours
— Interactive Webinar

 

Learn ways to think differently in key moments of change in the hiring process.

Bold Hiring is a reinvigorated approach to hiring we're taking at Turner. One that includes a sharper focus on bringing new ideas, perspectives, and experiences to teams across the company.

This interactive webinar will help you think about hiring in a new way and give you the tools and tips you need to make more of an impact with every opening you have.

You will:

  • Gain a better understanding of the full scope of the hiring process and how you can make the most of your role as a leader in it
  • Learn how making the decision to "choose different" can improve your team and make an important impact on Turner, too
