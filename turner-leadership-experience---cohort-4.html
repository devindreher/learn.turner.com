YOUR FACILITATORS

PAMELA DENNIS, Ph.d.

 

Dennis has worked with amazing leaders and their organizations for over 35 years - from global Fortune 100 firms like GE, Merck Pharmaceuticals, Wyeth, Telstra Communications, BHPbilliton, and JP Morgan to emerging companies and small, closely-held partnerships. Dennis knows the challenges of diverse industries in growth times and downturns.

 

Dennis was part of the core faculty and consulting team in twelve of the GE businesses launching and supporting major transformation initatives under Jack Welch and later Jeff Immelt as well as working with future CEOs of Google (Eric Schmidt) and GM (Mary Barra) in their change initiatives.

 

Dennis built and led an international consulting firm that received recognition for its own growth and impact - Mercury 100 Fastest Growing Colorado Companies and Boulder Top 25 Women Owned Companies before divesting her interests after 20 years.

 

Dennis is proud to be affiliated with the Leeds School of Business Women's Council at the University of Colorado which supports development women in leadership. She also serves on community non-profit boards and enjoys speaking at international and national organizations on leadership and change. She recently published her first book, Exit Signs: The Expressway to Selling Your Business with Pride and Profit (Sustainable Business Press, Portland, OR, 2015).

 

Her alma maters include a Ph.D. in organization development and education from the University of Colorado, an M.A. from the University of California, Santa Barbara, and a B.S. from California State Polytechnic, San Luis Obispo.

 

Dennis lives in Boulder, Colorado and San Diego, California. She enjoys world travel, not enough golf, and the joys of four wonderful grandchildren.

ron gager

GAGER & ASSOCIATES

 

Since founding Gager & Associates in 1987, Ron has had the pleasure of working with a wide range of outstanding organizations such as The Walt Disney Company, Merck Pharmaceuticals, General Electric, General Motors, JP Morgan, NASA, Morgan Stanley, and General Mills. Gager has worked closely with leaders at all levels - from C-suite executives to front-line supervisors - and continues to learn with them and from them every day.

 

During his decade-long engagement as faculty at General Electric's Leadership Development Institute and a similar program at GM University, Gager was instrumental in developing leadership programs in change management, organizational agility, and process improvement. His firm employs a "virtual team" model which has allowed Gager to mentor many young consultants over the last three decades - a role he still cherishes.

 

Gager earned his Masters in Education from the University of Colorado at Builder and an undergraduate B.S. from Central Michigan University. He serves as guest lecturer at the Leeds School of Business at the University of Colorado and is a long-standing faculty member of Roaring Fork Leadership, an Aspen-based community leadership development program.

 

Gager and his family live in Boulder, Colorado where he fuels his passions for cycling and fly fishing.

 

Ron Gager - My leadership journey.

Cathy "Gruffie" Clough

Clough & Associates

 

As president of Clough & Associates, Gruffie's work in the areas of leadership and organizational development, process design, and change management includes a unique mixture of assistance to leaders and organizations, both nationally and internationally, in the private and public sectors. As a trusted advisor, Gruffie works with leaders and teams to enhance the work that they do and produce measurable, sustainable outcomes.

 

Gruffie has served as a business coach and faculty members for General Motor's change management strategies, Go Fast! and Leading Change. She has facilitated multiple General Electric Work-Out and Change Acceleration programs and has worked with Federal Express' PIERCE Program. Additional claims include Ingersoll-Rand, Merck Pharmaceuticals, Telstra Communications, Kaiser Permanente, The Walt Disney Company,  World Vision, the American Cancer Society, and The Rockefeller Foundation.

 

Gruffie is also the co-author of Facilitating Partnerships, a change model and training guide used to increase team members' interaction, problem solving, and decision-making skills. She lives with her husband in Denver, Colorado and finds herself running, cycling, hiking, climbing in her free time.
