360 Leadership Agility Profile

A 360-degree feedback assessment is exactly what it sounds like - a full-circle view on how you are perceived.
You'll get feedback from people up (your manager), down (direct reports), and around (your peers) you. The data collected allows you to receive real information about the perceptions others hold about your performance rather than just your own (though that's also included).
While many of these types of assessments exist, we're using a tool that leans-in to many of the key leadership qualities needed to be successful. The Agility 360 focuses on Anticipating change, Generating confidence, Initiating action, Liberate thinking, Evaluate results - how agile are you?
The feedback you receive will help you craft your own development plan by identifying short and long-term goals with actionable plans to achieve them.
GET STARTED:
You must work with your Human Resources Generalist and/or Turner People Development to use this tool for your leadership development.
Send us a note if you're interested.
