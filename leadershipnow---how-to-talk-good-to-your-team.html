How to Talk 'Good' To Your Team

In our industry, communication is everything. It's also a key leadership skill.

This session will help you understand different communication styles and preferences so that you can communicate more effectively with your team and with your leadership.
You will:
- Explore communication styles and preferences and how to apply them to your team and leader communication
- Learn strategies for communicating with your team and your leadership
- Create your own personal leadership communication plan
