Emotional Intelligence (EQ)

The EQ assessment is a terrific tool to drive conversation and to enhance self-awareness around the emotional behaviors that hold us back from performing at our best.

The tool also helps pinpoint EQ strategies that increase emotional intelligence. This assessment is individual-focused, but will also offer positive impact on team dynamics and interaction when used with a team.
