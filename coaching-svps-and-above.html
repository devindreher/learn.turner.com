Coaching: SVPs and Above


Step What Happens
Step 1: Identify the Need You or an executive sponsor identify a need for an external certified coach.

Your leader discusses and initiates the coaching request with your HR partner.

Your HR partner informs Turner People Development of specific coaching needs, desired outcomes, situational context, personality, location, and budget. Average costs are anywhere from USD $15,000 - $25,000 for a 6-month engagement.
Step 2: Select a Coach Turner People Development provides 2-3 potential coach bios based on location, need, coaching experience, and available budget.

You then interview potential coaches for the best fit for your needs.

Your HR partner and Turner People Development offer high-level coaching objectives and terms.

Turner People Development facilitates contracting between the coach/coach vendor and Legal.
Step 3: Engage! You and your coach begin regular meetings - usually twice per month - and finalize objectives early on while sharing with your executive sponsor/leader and HR partner.

Check-in meetings are held at the beginning, mid-point, and end of the engagement with your leader.

The coach may conduct a live 360 / key stakeholder interviews including your manager, peers, and direct reports. Results are summarized.
Step 4: Close & Follow-Up Turner People Development contacts the coach at the close of the engagement to confirm completion.

A final meeting takes place between you, your coach, and your manager.

Exit surveys are completed by you and your coach.

Turner People Development follows up with a 6 month check-in with you HR partner to discuss sustained behavior change and encourages check-ins with your leader on your progress.
