360 Organizational Agility Profile

Similar to the Agility 360 for individuals, organizations have the opportunity to use this assessment for their group.

This tool focuses on five key areas needed to propel an organization forward: it's ability to...

Anticipating change

Generating confidence

Initiating action

Liberate thinking

Evaluate results

Various stakeholders will weigh in on the organization's current state in these areas. The data gleaned will be instrumental in the organization's future strategic planning and leadership development.

Logistics

  • 20 minutes to complete
  • $350 + $15 per rater

Getting Started

You must work with your Human Resources Generalist and/or Turner People Development to use this tool for your leadership development. Send us a note if you're interested.
