Verbal 360 Feedback

A Verbal 360 feedback is our way of customizing the process for you.

It allows us to meet 1:1 with those all around you — up (your manager), down, (your direct reports), and around (your peers).

Similar to a typical 360–degree feedback, we'll collect data which will allow you to receive information about the perceptions others hold about your performance rather your own.

The feedback you receive will help you craft your individual development plan by identifying short and long-term development goals with actionable plans to achieve them.

Logistics

  • 30 minutes per session
  • Free

Getting Started

You must work with your Human Resources Generalist and/or Turner People Development to use this tool for your leadership development. Send us a note if you're interested.
