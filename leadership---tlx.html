Turner Leadership Experience

The Turner Leadership Experience is designed to build a cadre of senior leaders with the knowledge, capabilities, and confidence to re-imagine TV and drive a culture of more fun, less fear, and all in.

Program objectives:

To promote senior leadership that excels in:

  • Strategy alignment and execution
  • Fosting engagement and accountability
  • Building agile organizations that are innovative, lean, and fast

Who Is This For?

Nominated VPs and directors.
