LeadershipNow

The LeadershipNow suite is designed to develop people leaders' ability to drive the change we need.

The suite encompasses an array of virtual and classroom offerings open to anyone with people leadership responsibilities - either on a org chart or in practice. Read our FAQ for more information.

Turner Leaders Are...

Accountable

Design strategic and actionable plans.
Lead teams that deliver results.
Drive performance with actionable feedback.
Trust and empower their teams to deliver.

Agile

Iterative and flexible.
Promote a constant state of reinvention.
Build teams that thrive on innovation.
Grow and nurture team members.

Innovative

Takes initiative and embraces risk.
Encourages bold decision making and hiring.
Creates an environment for creative thinking.
Challenges their teams to focus on priorities.
Encourages shedding of non-essential work.

Collaborative

Breaks down silos and builds partnerships.
Seeks diverse perspectives.
Thinks and acts globally.
Makes proactive communications a priority.
