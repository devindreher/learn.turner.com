StrengthsFinder 2.0

StrengthsFinders is an assessment used to tap into an individual's natural talents and strengths to increase satisfaction and engagement.

Going against the norm, this assessment focuses on developing strengths rather than fixing shortcomings. The tool comes with hundreds of strategies for applying your strengths and finding opportunities for doing what do you do best every day.

The StrengthsFinders assessment is individual-focused, but learnings can be applied at the team level for discussion around opportunities for greater performance and collaboration.

Getting Started

Purchase the StrengthsFinders 2.0 book.

Find the unique StrengthsFinder key inside the red insert at the end of the book.

Scratch off the foil to revel your key.

Go to strengths.gallup.com and follow the instructions for inputting your key.
