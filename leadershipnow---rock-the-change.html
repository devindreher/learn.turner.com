Rock the Change

There's one thing that's constant here — change.

We'll explore how to ensure balance and focus during periods of change, learn to influence up, and how to provide support to your team.

You will:

  • Understand the implications of change initiatives on people and your role in guiding employees through change transitions
  • Identify what's in/out of control in change and how to influence up to support it
  • Apply change leadership tools and concepts in our environment

Logistics:

– 2 Hours

– Interactive webinar

 

Use When:

There's too much change

Your team is resistant to change

You need to guide your team through change
