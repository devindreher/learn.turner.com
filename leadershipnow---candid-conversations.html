Candid Conversations

Use When:

You're unsure how to give tough feedback.
You're building a culture of candor.
You want to have regular check-ins with your team.

Logistics:

— 2 Hours
— Interactive Webinar

 

Just say it!

Learn how to put this idea skillfully into practice for the benefit of others — without being a jerk.

Also, learn how to build a culture of candor through the many ways we interact with outs. We're putting emphasis on frequent and meaningful conversations where everyone gets timely and candid feedback in order to do our best.

You will:

  • Learn ways to build a culture of candor through interacting with others
  • Learn a simple approach for giving feedback — for both praise and criticism
  • Apply feedback skills through practice with common scenarios
  • Contribute to Turner by building a culture of candor
