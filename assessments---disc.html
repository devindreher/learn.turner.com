DiSC

DiSC is a personal assessment tool used to drive conversation and awareness around communication styles and workplace behaviors.

While there are several tools similar in nature, DiSC stands out from others because of its focus on workplace behaviors rather than personality. You'll receive a rich report with strategies for increased performance after taking the assessment. DiSC can be used in a team setting for discussion around strategies for effective communication, handling conflict, and problem-solving keeping in mind the differences in communication styles and behaviors.
