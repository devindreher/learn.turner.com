StandOut

StandOut is an action-oriented assessment that reveals the personal edge that will help you win at work.
The assessment measures you on 9 strength roles and reveals your top 2 which are your edge - your competitive advantage. You'll receive an extensive coaching report immediately offering practical ideas to find your "winning edge" at work. This assessment can be used as a team for discussion around group implications and opportunities for greater performance.
